Welcome to Saifi TV. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

### 1. Information Collection and Use

**Location Data**
The App may access your device's location to provide Prayer Times and Qibla direction.
- Location is used for these features on your device.
- We do not operate a Saifi TV server that stores your location for advertising.

**Personal Information**
We do not require you to create an account for core features.

**Local Storage**
Favorites, Zikr counts, notification preferences, and settings are stored only on your device.

### 2. Third-Party Services — YouTube API Services

The App uses YouTube API Services to list and play videos from approved channels via YouTube’s official embed player (YouTube controls and branding). By using video features you agree to:
- YouTube Terms of Service: https://www.youtube.com/t/terms
- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not download, rehost, or strip audio from YouTube content.

### 3. Notifications

Local reminders (prayer, Jummah, Hadith, Zikr, new video checks) may be scheduled on your device if you allow notifications.

### 4. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted at the hosted URL above.
