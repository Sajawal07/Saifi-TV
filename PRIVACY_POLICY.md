Privacy Policy for Saifi TV
===========================

Last updated: July 2026

Welcome to Saifi TV. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle, collect, and use your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

### 1. Information Collection and Use

**Location Data**
The App requires access to your device's location to provide accurate Prayer Times and Qibla direction. 
- This location data is processed **locally** on your device.
- We **do not** collect, transmit, store on our servers, or share your location data with any third parties.

**Personal Information**
We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to use the core features of the App.

### 2. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. 
- **YouTube API Services**: The App embeds YouTube videos. By watching these videos, you are subject to the Google Privacy Policy (http://www.google.com/policies/privacy) and YouTube Terms of Service (https://www.youtube.com/t/terms).

### 3. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

### 4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
