Privacy Policy

Saifi TV · Last updated: July 2026

Welcome to Saifi TV. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

1. Information we use

Location data. The App may access your device location to provide Prayer Times and Qibla direction. Location is processed on your device for these features. We do not operate a backend that stores your location for profiling or advertising.

Local storage. Favorites, Zikr counts, notification preferences, and similar settings are stored only on your device.

Accounts. The App does not require you to create an account for core features.

2. YouTube API Services

The App uses YouTube API Services to list videos from approved channels and play them through YouTube’s official embed player (with YouTube controls and branding). By using video features you agree to be bound by the:

  • YouTube Terms of Service
  • Google Privacy Policy

We do not download, rehost, or strip audio from YouTube content.

3. Notifications

If you allow notifications, the App may schedule local reminders (prayer times, Jummah, Hadith, Zikr, and new-video checks on your device). You can control these in the App’s Notifications settings and in system settings.

4. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

5. Contact

Questions about this policy: support@saifitv.app
